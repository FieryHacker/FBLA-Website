About Xavier Len

I was born in Alberta, Canada and, just like many others, I fell in love with the beautiful scenery of the Moraine Lake. It was then and there where I fell in love with photography. Capturing the moments of the simple nature scenes fueled a passion within me. Once I was out of college, I decided to move to California as many well-known influencers live there. It was so different in comparison to my hometown of Alberta; the entire state is nearly urbanized. With that, I also noticed another thing: everything here is so similar, everyone wanted to get that perfect photo, or get the newest thing; I didn’t see much originality. It has then when I fell in love with a type of contemporary photography known as Wabi Sabi, which places an importance on nature and imperfections, such ideas not very well known in the state that I currently live in. Wabi Sabi teaches those to find beauty and appreciation in what is natural and what is imperfect, for those have a uniqueness that could not be found in something perfect.
